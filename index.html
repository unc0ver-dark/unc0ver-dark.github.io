unc0ver dark
Join the dark side.


unc0ver dark is a UI improved fork of the popular jailbreak unc0ver, now bursting with less color. Not only does it sport a beautiful, OLED-compatible design, unc0ver dark also uses Sileo as its main package manager and downloads Substrate directly from the source.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about unc0ver dark.



What are the differences between unc0ver dark and normal unc0ver?

The main differences are the dark UI, apt 1.8, Sileo support (not required), and Substrate (which is downloaded from the source to comply with licenses).


What is Sileo?

Sileo is a more modern package manager that was made and designed for iOS 11 and up. It's a faster alternative to Cydia, with a more modern UI and speed enhancements (such as not going full screen when refreshing sources).


What is the Diatrus/Misceo repo?

The Diatrus/Misceo hosts essential packages needed for unc0ver dark to run, as well as numerous other utilities. It is like Elucubratus on unc0ver, electrarepo64 on Electra, or Telesphoreo on older jailbreaks. Do not add Elucubratus to your sources when using unc0ver dark.


How do I transfer my Cydia sources to Sileo?

Download Flame from Cydia and use it to export your sources list, then copy all of the sources and batch add them to Sileo.


Do I have to restore root filesystem to use unc0ver Dark?

Nope! Just simply reboot and rejailbreak with unc0ver Dark.


Can I remove Sileo?

Yes! Just uninstall it in Cydia and use Cydia as your main. If you want to re-install at any time, toggle the switch in the app to reinstall Sileo.
