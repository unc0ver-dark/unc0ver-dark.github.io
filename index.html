faq


will i need to restore rootfs to switch to this from u0 white?

no, you can switch to u0 dark by simply rebooting and jailbreaking with it


why is my cydia crashing on u0 dark?

cydown makes cydia crash, remove it to fix the problem


what is the diatrus/misceo repo?

diatrus/misceo hosts essential packages needed for u0 dark to run, as well as numerous other utilities; it is like elucubratus on u0 white, electrarepo64 on electra, or telesphoreo on older jailbreaks. do not add elucubratus to your sources when using u0 dark


what is sileo?

sileo is a more modern package manager made for ios 11 and up. it's a faster alternative to cydia with a more modern ui and some other quality of life changes (example: it doesn't go full screen when refreshing sources)


how do i transfer my cydia sources to sileo?

download flame from cydia and use it to export your sources list, then copy all of the sources and batch add them to sileo
